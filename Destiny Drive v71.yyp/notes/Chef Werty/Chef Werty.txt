Get three pickups while holding right click to get special abilities
If you give the chef a pickup, you don't get the pickup abilities yourself.

HEART HEART HEART
Heal (HP / 2).

SHIELD SHIELD SHIELD
Converts all hp into shields.

POWERSHIELD SHIELD SHIELD
Destroys all bullets on screen.

HEART SHIELD POWERSHIELD
Hearts go into shields, shields into powershields, and powershields into hp.

ENERGY ENERGY ENERGY
Sends a lightning bolt that hurts enemies a bit. If playing as alien, fully charge other item.

CHEST CHEST CHEST
Spawns each pickup.
